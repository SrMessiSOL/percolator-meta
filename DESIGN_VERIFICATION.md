## Phase 0 Decision

Proceed with fixes for F1, F2, F3, and O1 only.

Do not file F4 as a current-master vulnerability because the vulnerable `mint_reward` route exists only in the local spike branch, not in `338c31d`.

Do not file O2/O3 as standalone vulnerabilities. They are useful hardening notes, but they do not meet the "REAL BUG" threshold after design-intent verification.
